Montauk, New York: The End

Montauk is located on the easternmost tip of Long Island, New York. Known for its scenic beaches,cozy restaurants, bars and ice cream shops, Montauk is a great getaway for New York City and greater Long Island natives looking for a weekend retreat in the sand and sun.
